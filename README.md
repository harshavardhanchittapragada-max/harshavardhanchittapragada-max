### 🔍 Crime Against Women in India — Data Analysis
Analyzed 15+ years of NCRB government data across 35 states and 10+ crime categories.
- 3 states (UP, Maharashtra, Rajasthan) account for ~42% of all reported crimes nationally
- Higher literacy states show higher reporting rates — not higher crime — revealing a key bias in how official statistics are read
- Documented consistent 6–8% YoY increase in reported cases from 2010 to 2020
